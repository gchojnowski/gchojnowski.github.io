I provide independent scientific consulting at the interface of **AI-based protein structure prediction** and **integrative structural biology**, supporting academic and biotech projects that require careful model interpretation, method development, or scalable computational workflows.

My work combines experience in experimental structure determination (cryo-EM, macromolecular crystallography, small-angle scattering) with large-scale structure prediction and validation using modern AI-based methods.
